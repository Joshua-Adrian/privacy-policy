Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: March 2024

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request the deletion of your personal data from Sunroom Assistant in any of the following ways:

Option 1: Direct Message on Instagram

Send a direct message to @sunroomstudios on Instagram with the text "Request data deletion". Our team will process your request within 48 hours.

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to sunroomstudios@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Please include:

  • Your Instagram username
  • The email address associated with your bookings (if any)
  • A statement requesting data deletion

Option 3: Contact Form

Visit our studio or contact us at:

63 Hillview Avenue, 10-13C
Singapore 669569

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon your request, we will delete:

  • Your message history with our chatbot
  • Your booking history and preferences
  • Any personal information stored in our system
  • Your contact information

Timeframe

We will process your deletion request within:

  • 48 hours for initial confirmation
  • 7 days for complete data deletion

You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

Data Retention Requirements

Please note that we may be required to retain certain information:

  • For legal compliance purposes
  • To resolve disputes
  • To enforce our terms and agreements

Such information will be retained only as long as necessary for these purposes.
